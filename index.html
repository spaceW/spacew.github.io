About Hui Wu

I am a computer vision scientist at IBM Research AI, currently focused on understanding and developing deep learning algorithms for real-world applications such as visual recognition and fashion design. Beyond my work in computer vision, I am also interested in how the research on human intelligence and machine intelligence can learn from each other, and how we can develop beneficial and responsible AI tools to better our future.

Prior to IBM, I received my PhD in Computer Science from UNC Charlotte, where I worked with Dr. Richard Souvenir (2011-2015) on the subject of manifold learning based machine learning techniques and their applications in computer vision and medical image analysis.

Contact me: wuhu _at_ us.ibm.com
